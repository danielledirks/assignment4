Art has the power to unite and inspire even in the face of uncertainty and adversity; it can please the soul as much as it can ignite a movement. I wish my art to be provocative: to address the taboo issues in society. The things I create can be interpreted as both empowering and thought-provoking, pushing people to question what they see, what they know, and to open their minds to the world around them.

Currently finishing out my senior year at Nazareth College.

- Danielle Marie Dirks
